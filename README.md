# Power-BI-Financial-Performance-Dashboard-for-Flexy-Fitness-Company-Revenue-Expense-Profit-Analysis

## Project objective
The objective of this project is to develop an interactive Power BI Financial Performance Dashboard that provides a comprehensive analysis of Flexy Fitness Company's financial data. The dashboard aims to monitor key financial KPIs, including revenue, expenses, gross profit, net profit, COGS, OPEX, and EBIT, while enabling users to evaluate performance across different business lines. By offering trend analysis, expense breakdowns, profitability insights, and dynamic filtering by month and quarter, the dashboard supports data-driven decision-making, identifies cost optimization opportunities, and helps improve overall financial performance.

### Process
- Collected and imported the financial dataset into Power BI Desktop.
- Cleaned and transformed the data using Power Query by handling missing values, correcting data types, and removing inconsistencies.
- Created a data model by establishing relationships between fact and dimension tables.
- Developed DAX measures to calculate key financial KPIs, including Revenue, Sales, COGS, OPEX, Gross Profit, Net Profit, EBIT, Gross Margin %, Net Margin %, and OPEX Ratio.
- Designed an interactive Financial Overview dashboard to monitor overall business performance, revenue trends, and business-line contributions.
- Built an Expense Analysis dashboard to analyze operating expenses, COGS, Interest & Tax, expense categories, and cost distribution across business lines.
- Created a Profit Analysis dashboard to evaluate Gross Profit, EBIT, Net Profit, profit margins, and profitability trends by month and business line.
- Added interactive slicers for Month and Quarter along with navigation buttons to enable seamless filtering and report navigation.
- Applied consistent formatting, charts, KPI cards, tables, and visual elements to improve readability and user experience.
- Validated the dashboard calculations and insights to ensure accuracy and deliver actionable financial intelligence for business decision-making.

### Project Insights
- The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the company's financial performance by consolidating Revenue, Sales, Expenses, COGS, Gross Profit, EBIT, and Net Profit into a single reporting solution.
- Business line comparisons help identify the highest-performing and lowest-performing segments based on revenue, profitability, and expense ratios.
- Monthly trend analysis highlights fluctuations in revenue, expenses, and profits, making it easier to detect seasonal patterns and performance changes.
- Expense analysis reveals the major cost drivers, including COGS, Operating Expenses (OPEX), and Interest & Tax, enabling better cost management.
- Profitability metrics such as Gross Margin, Net Margin, EBIT Margin, and OPEX Ratio provide a clear understanding of overall financial efficiency.
- Interactive filters for Month and Quarter allow users to perform dynamic analysis and compare financial performance across different time periods.
- Category-wise expense breakdowns help management identify opportunities to reduce unnecessary spending and improve operational efficiency.
- Revenue and profit comparisons across business lines support strategic decision-making by highlighting areas with the greatest growth potential.
- KPI cards provide an instant summary of key financial metrics, enabling executives to monitor business performance at a glance.
- The dashboard transforms raw financial data into actionable insights, helping stakeholders make informed, data-driven financial and operational decisions.

## Final Conclusion
This Power BI Financial Performance Dashboard delivers a comprehensive analysis of the company's financial health by integrating revenue, expenses, and profitability into an interactive reporting solution. Through dynamic visualizations, KPI tracking, and business line comparisons, the dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor financial performance, identify cost optimization opportunities, and evaluate profit margins with ease. Interactive filters for months and quarters enhance analytical flexibility, allowing users to explore trends and make meaningful comparisons. Overall, this project demonstrates how Power BI can transform raw financial data into actionable business insights, empowering organizations to make informed, data-driven decisions that improve operational efficiency and long-term profitability.
